  • Gameplay: A paragraph describing the actions the player can perform, the system dynamics, and the core mechanic. Include a concise explanation of the prototypeâ€™s inputs and their expected effects (how to play). You can also describe game play that is not in the prototype. You may include mock-up images for parts of the game not in the prototype.
    The eraser follows the player's mouse's horizontal position. Clicking launches the ball upward. The ball will bounce off the eraser and edges of the screen except for the bottom. If the ball falls off the bottom of the screen it will reset attached to the eraser. The chemical bottles will only "open" (dissapear) if they are hit from above (or at a high point on the side). If all of the bottles are opened the game will reset.
    Tip: If the ball hits the eraser off-center it will bounce slightly that direction.
  • Strategies: What player strategies do you expect will be effective at playing this game?
    Patience and angular prediction. If you do not get a good angle you may need to wait until the ball ends up in a good spot again. The ability to know where the ball is going to go after it bounces is essential.
