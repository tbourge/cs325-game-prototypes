  • Gameplay: A paragraph describing the actions the player can perform, the system dynamics, and the core mechanic. Include a concise explanation of the prototypeâ€™s inputs and their expected effects (how to play). You can also describe game play that is not in the prototype. You may include mock-up images for parts of the game not in the prototype.
    At the beginning of the game the player can click START to begin the game or read the instructions on the bottom.
    Once they click START they can click LIFT to increase their power bar. Each time they click LIFT the bar will increase slightly, however, the bar will
    constantly drain. The goal is to keep the bar halfway filled. If the player does not keep the bar sufficiently filled then they will drop the barbell and
    lose. If they keep the bar adequately full they will lift the barbell above their head and enter phase 2. Before phase 2 the player can read the updated
    instructions on the bottom of the screen and can click START when they are ready to begin. In phase 2 the player must click the HOLD button when the bar
    is halfway full. If they click when the bar is green then they will succeed in holding the barbell and will win. If they click when the bar is red then
    they will drop the barbell and lose. When the game ends, with either a win or loss, a reset button will appear that the player can click to restart the
    game.
