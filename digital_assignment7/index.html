    Once the player clicks start the cards will appear faceup for 3 seconds. After the 3 seconds are up the cards will flip over and the player tokens will appear on the top left card. The blue player can move their token with the arrow keys (each movement requires its own button press) and can flip the card their token is on with control. The red player moves with WASD and flips cards with C. The green player moves with IJKL and flips cards with N. The yellow player moves with 5123 and flips cards with 0. A flipped card will become highlighted with the player's color. If the player selects a card while they already have a card selected then the cards will remain flipped and highlighted if they match, this will award two points to that player. If the cards do not match then they will become unhighlighted and will flip back over after one second. If a player flips over a card and a different player flips over the matching card then the 2nd player will acquire the match and earn one point. The player who gets the most points wins. The game ends when the timer runs out or all the cards are matched.
